Greenlight was made to give back to the 42 community.
(1)The plants were selected with purpose; edible plants for the local cantina and air purifying plants for the lab.
(2)Parts of the structure were made through 3D modeling and printing(hooks, shower cap, skirt)
(3)Arduino and Raspberry Pi are programmed to detect humidity and temperature levels. Click the Pi logo to see real time data.
(4)LED lights are used to grow as well as notify when the temperature, humidity, or water levels are too high or low. Red for the first two and green for the third.
P.S. Hover over the lettuce.
Coming slowly from the left works best.
